From Reporting to Direction: How ESGpro Makes Sustainability Reporting Workable

For many organisations, sustainability reporting remains a yearly struggle. Endless Excel sheets, scattered questionnaires, overlapping audits; all necessary, but rarely efficient. According to Sebastiaan De Block, CEO of The Ecological Entrepreneur, that's exactly what the new platform ESGpro aims to change.

"We saw it time and again with our clients: companies with the best intentions, but without overview. The strategy was clear on paper, yet reporting stalled in data, definitions and formulas. Every year, the process started again from scratch. ESGpro was born from that reality."

Tussentitel van artikel

Many companies simply don't know where to start with ESG reporting. ESGpro begins with a digital pre-assessment: a short questionnaire that automatically identifies the relevant, mandatory and voluntary ESG topics based on sector, size and activity.

"In five minutes you know what's mandatory, what's optional and where the biggest impact lies. It gives companies direction — before they even start collecting data." — Sebastiaan De Block

"Our ambition is to be the best in class. We've accomplished a great deal, but we recognise that we're still on a journey. Perfection isn't our aim – continuous improvement is. We embrace being 90% there, as it leaves room for growth, innovation, and adaptation. We know that the path to sustainability is ever evolving, and we're committed to evolving with it."

— Sebastiaan & Marit – founders of The Ecological Entrepreneur

Tussentitel van artikel

After the pre-assessment, ESGpro gathers the required information through its integrated partners. Only missing data need to be collected and entered manually. The platform recognises, interprets and links data to the correct report sections, fully compliant with the VSME standard. It also provides calculations for CO₂ emissions (Scopes 1 & 2), social indicators and benchmarking based on anonymised market data.

"Companies can finally demonstrate progress objectively to clients, investors and banks, not to charge more, but to maintain their position in a rapidly changing market."

Tussentitel van artikel

We don't want companies to change how they work. Quite the opposite, we connect to their existing partners and data. Thanks to integrations with SBB, Acerta and Fluvius, ESGpro is practical and applicable from day one.

"Raf Dom, CEO of SBB Accountants & Advisors, confirms this approach: 'An accountant holds 90% of the data a company needs for sustainability reporting. By linking that data automatically, we make reporting finally logical and efficient.'"
