Overview

What is it?

The Ecological Entrepreneur was born out of one simple belief: we can make sustainability self-evident by bringing economy and ecology together. This is necessary to maintain our current level of prosperity and well-being and to protect our natural world. To provide solutions for both questions, our company focusses on three activities: guiding organisations towards sustainability, scaling the impact of ecological solutions, and capturing your impact via performance dashboarding. Crossovers happen a lot between all activities, as eco-solutions might help other companies reach their goal. Thus, we bring economy and ecology together.

The case for reporting

Why a VSME report?

A robust sustainability strategy is essential for long-term success. We guide your organisation in moving from stand-alone actions to a fully-fledged ESG strategy. By defining clear sustainability goals, exploring future scenarios, and establishing key performance indicators (KPIs), we ensure that your initiatives are integrated into your overall business strategy, delivering both environmental, social and economic benefits.

Questions?

After the pre-assessment, ESGpro gathers the required information through its integrated partners. Only missing data need to be collected and entered manually. The platform recognises, interprets and links data to the correct report sections, fully compliant with the VSME standard. It also provides calculations for CO₂ emissions (Scopes 1 & 2), social indicators and benchmarking based on anonymised market data.

"Companies can finally demonstrate progress objectively to clients, investors and banks, not to charge more, but to maintain their position in a rapidly changing market."
