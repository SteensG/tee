Our story

Who we are

The Ecological Entrepreneur was born out of one simple belief: we can make sustainability self-evident by bringing economy and ecology together. This is necessary to maintain our current level of prosperity and well-being and to protect our natural world.

Our company focusses on three activities: guiding organisations towards sustainability, scaling the impact of ecological solutions, and capturing your impact via performance dashboarding. Crossovers happen a lot between all activities, as eco-solutions might help other companies reach their goal. Thus, we bring economy and ecology together.

Our purpose

A leading sustainability consultancy

The Ecological Entrepreneur is a leading sustainability consultancy firm that supports businesses, sector federations, and organisations in their transition to sustainable and compliant operations. We help develop long-term sustainability strategies, balancing ecological, social, and economic factors to ensure profitable, responsible growth.

Our services include ESG strategy development, impact assessments, compliance reporting, and the implementation of sustainable practices. Through engagement workshops and tailored analyses, we foster internal alignment on sustainability goals and provide actionable insights to meet regulatory standards.

Vision

Creating a positive impact on the environment, on an ecological, social, and economical level. Organisations should create a sustainable profit, by balancing their ecological and social costs, and become profitable on all levels. One metric cannot overrule other metrics.

Mission

Making sustainability self-evident by bringing together ecology and economy. By guiding companies on their path to becoming sustainable and climate neutral businesses. And scaling the impact of sustainable solutions and products through commercial growth.
