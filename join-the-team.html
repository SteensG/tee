Carbon Footprint Consultant

12 January 2026

Domain

Sustainability

Type

Fulltime

Location

Antwerp

Posted

12 January 2026

Would you like to contribute to sustainability and make a difference in the transition to more sustainable business practices? As a Carbon Consultant, you will conduct detailed CO₂ analyses and contribute to the development of net zero strategies for various organisations. Are you ready to guide companies towards a greener future? If so, we'd love to meet you!

We are seeking energetic and motivated colleagues to play a crucial role in reducing our clients' carbon footprints. You will daily perform key roles in conducting detailed CO₂ analyses and developing various reduction and offset scenarios under the guidance of a Sustainability Consultant. Your expertise will help companies identify opportunities for CO₂ reduction and support them in transitioning to more sustainable operations. You are precise, experienced in managing multiple projects simultaneously, and continually supported in your personal and professional development to enhance your competencies and skills.

Your Role
You will conduct CO₂ analyses, develop net-zero strategies, support clients in sustainability transitions, and collaborate with our team of consultants on impactful projects across Belgium and beyond.
Why are you a good fit?
You have a strong interest in sustainability and carbon management. You are analytical, detail-oriented, and comfortable working with data. You communicate clearly and can translate complex concepts for clients. Experience with CO₂ reporting tools is a plus.
Why are we a good fit?
We offer a dynamic, purpose-driven work environment where your contributions directly reduce carbon footprints across industries. You'll grow fast, work with a passionate team, and make real impact from day one.
