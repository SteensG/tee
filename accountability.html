About our approach

Since The Ecological Entrepreneur was founded, we have been driven by a holistic vision of sustainability. It's our mission to harmonise economy with ecology and society. We want to create a positive impact on all three levels, and help others to do the same, so we can safeguard our current quality of life and ensure a fair and fulfilling life for everyone – today and in the future.

Our yearly sustainability report encourages us to reflect on our past efforts, track our progress, and cast our eye towards the future. Our goal is to create transparency and inspire action – within our own organisation, amongst stakeholders, and clients.

Our targets

What we aim for

Our sustainability strategy consists of long- and short-term goals. The roadmap we developed to reach those goals is extensive and highly detailed, and more importantly, subject to change if need be. However, we have defined a few overarching goals and priorities we are determined to achieve no matter what.

By 2025, our goal is to reach climate neutrality, and by 2030, to become carbon negative.

Inclusivity, employee well-being, and ethical supplier and customer relations are non-negotiable.

We will implement policies that align with our commitment to ethical business practices and transparency.
