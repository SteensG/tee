Why now?

From today's position to tomorrow's impact

The European sustainability landscape is changing rapidly. The recent Omnibus Act on CSRD obligations is still under discussion, but customers, banks and investors continue to set higher expectations in terms of transparency and responsibility. Understanding what truly matters, and where sustainability adds business value, is now key to staying relevant.

With this quick scan, you'll discover:

Where sustainability creates the most economic and strategic impact

How to reduce risks, costs and exposure

The next steps to strengthen your sustainability performance

Assessment areas

Discover your sustainability priorities

The Sustainability Quick Scan helps companies assess their current sustainability performance across five core areas:

CO₂ impact

Measure and understand your carbon footprint across all business activities and supply chain.

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)

Evaluate the full environmental impact of your products and services from cradle to grave.

Reporting (VSME)

Assess your readiness for sustainability reporting under the VSME standard and beyond.

Social impact analysis

Understand your organisation's social footprint: employees, communities, and supply chain partners.

EcoVadis certification

Gauge your current EcoVadis readiness and identify which areas need attention before certification.
